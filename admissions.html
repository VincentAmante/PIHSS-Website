Admissions Overview

We understand that choosing the right school for your child is a huge decision, so our admissions process is simple, straightforward and designed to give assurance to you and your child that PIHSS is the right choice for your family.

Parents seeking admission for their children should contact the school during Jan-Feb.

Please refer to age requirements for each class in the end of page.

Please note that the admission depends on the availability of seats. Applicants are contacted only if seats are available.

Admissions Process

  • KG1 admission: Assessment Interviews KG2 - Grade 10 admission: Entrance tests in English, Mathematics & Science
  • Answer papers will NOT be shown to parents
  • Parents will be notified by admin office via SMS/phone call/website for the test date
  • Successful applicants will be notified by the Admin office via SMS/phone call/website and pasted on the schoolâ€™s notice board

Fee Structure

Fee is applicable for 10 months i.e. from April to March (Excluding July & August). In case if a student joins during the session, fee will be applicable from start of the academic year i.e. from April.

Class Fee
KG 1 - Grade 3 AED 4,500 / Annum
Grade 4 - Grade 5 AED 4,700 / Annum
Grade 6 - Grade 8 AED 5,200 / Annum
Grade 9 AED 5,800 / Annum
Grade 10 AED 6,000 / Annum
Grade 11 - Grade 12 AED 8,000 / Annum

School tuition fees are subject to variation after approval of the U.A.E Ministry of Education.

Note:

  • Federal Board Registration Fee will be charged for students of grade 9 & 11 students.
  • Federal Board Examination Fee will be charged for students of Grade 9 - 12.
  • The fee rate is subject to enhancement anytime after it is duly approved by the Ministry of Education.
  • Fee should be paid in advance before 10th of every month.
  • Non-payment of fee will result in the name to be forwarded to the Ministry of Education for cancellation of admission as per rules.

Discounts

Following discounts are available in tuition fee:

  • 50% discount on tuition fee for orphans subject to provision of required documents.
  • (KG 1 - Grade 5) AED20 discount on tuition fee for 3rd child onwards (applicable to the student studying in lowest class)
  • (Grade 6 - Grade 10) AED30 discount on tuition fee for 3rd child onwards
  • (Grade 11 - Grade 12) AED60 discount on tuition fee if 2 or more children are in grade 11 or 12.

Refunds

Refunds will be done as follows:

  • Full refund if the student does not attend the class.
  • 1 month fee will be deducted if the student attends the school for 2 weeks or less.
  • 2 months fee will be deducted if the student attends the school for more than 2 weeks and less than 1 month.
  • 3 months fee will be deducted if the student attends the school for more than 1 month.
