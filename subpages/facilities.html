Facilities

Auditorium

The institute is equipped with the audio-visual auditorium facilities. It is a commodious auditorium with a capacity to accommodate more than 500 people. The hall stands witness to the blossoming of hidden talents. The auditorium has well equipped and fully designed light and sound system.

Clinic

There are two fully equipped clinics to provide first-aid and basic health facilities to the students. Trained and experienced nurses are on duty all through the school hours. They work under the guidance of a qualified doctor. The clinic staff coordinate with the UAE Ministry of Health to implement various programmes devised by them. They are responsible to admit the student to the hospital in case of an emergency and update the parents about the health care of the child when necessary.

Junior's Activities

An activity room is every child’s favourite place and this spacious and well equipped room makes learning more fun filled and curiosity centered. The child is able to home his adaptability skills through interactive play and hands on activities. Activity room comprises of art corner, audio visual area, play corner and fairy tale corner, where children can mesmerize themselves in the fantasy world. The concept of activity room is introduction of an innovative and life changing learning experience for the children.

Laboratories

Spacious Biology, Chemistry, Computer & Physics laboratories in the Boys and Girls sections and Home Economics laboratory in the Girls section are well-equipped to meet the requirements of practicals for these subjects. Highly qualified science and social science teachers and laboratory assistants supervise and guide the students in their practicals.

Libraries

No educational institution is complete without a good, well stocked library, nor can education be complete without inculcating healthy reading habits. The school has two well stocked, spacious, well lit and ventilated libraries which house about 10,000 books on varied subjects. Children have their individual preferences when it comes to reading. To ensure that every child gets to read what he likes, the school sends group of teachers to Book Fairs to select titles which are then added to the libraries after the necessary review of their appropriateness. The libraries are managed by qualified librarians.

Sports

Sports form an integral part of the education system. Sports play a pivotal role in the makeup of a young athlete. Participating in sports help students to understand the meaning of the term “Team Work”. Sports help to inculcate discipline. It helps the students to be active, healthy and strong. Sports facilities for Cricket, Football, Basketball, Badminton and Volleyball are provided for both Boys & Girls.

Transport

The School has its own fleet of air-conditioned buses to provide safe and secure transportation.

Please Note:

  • Parents should escort the students to and from the bus stop.
  • Parents should preferably choose a bus stop where a group of students are available.
  • Parents having any complaint against the school transport can contact the transport incharge.
  • The students will not be permitted to avail the school transport services under the following circumstances:
    1. Persistent misbehaviour with the staff or students in bus.
    2. Being habitually late.
    3. Intentionally damaging the school bus and other fixtures.
    4. Activities that may endanger the students in the bus.
    5. Defaulting in bus fee payment.
