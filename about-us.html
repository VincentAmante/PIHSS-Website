Mission

Within a safe and secure school environment we seek:

  • To provide equitable quality education to all students through teamwork and cooperation of all stakeholders.
  • To equip our students for the world of today and tomorrow.
  • To widen horizon of opportunities for students to achieve their goals
  • To inculcate in our students the noble sense of fairness, tolerance and respect for other cultures while preserving our golden Islamic traditions.
  • To build up their personalities to make them responsible, confident and productive citizens.

Pakistan Islamia Higher Secondary School, Sharjah (previously known as Pakistan Muslim High School) was established by Mr. Akhtar Mahmood Kiyani in September 1974, under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Dr. Sultan Bin Mohammad Al-Qasmi, the ruler of Sharjah and member of the Supreme Council of U.A.E. Initially the classes began with around 150 students at the Old Al-Qasimiah Camp. The original Board of Governors was under the chairmanship of Dr. Ghulam Hussain Jaffer while Mr. Gulzar Ahmed Qureshi served as secretary to the Board.

The present campus was built on the plot of land donated by His Highness Dr. Sheikh Muhammad Al-Qasmi, member of the Supreme Council and the Ruler of Sharjah, in March 1977. Anew Board of Governors was constituted under the chairmanship of Mr. Abdul Rehman Bukhatir. The school was upgrated to Higher Secondary level. Group Captain Sulaiman Kiyani (late) was appointed as the new Principal. In 1978 the responsibility of the institute was assigned to Mr. Saeed-Ur-Rehman who rendered his services for longest tenure, i.e.1978 - 1988.

In 1990 the school was put under the administrative control of the Consulate General of Pakistan. His Excellency Aftab Hussain Syed took charge of the school as Chairman of the newly Constituted Board of Governors. The school took quantum leaps in its progress under the new management. The strength of students increased considerably. At present Mr. Abdul Qudus Saggu is the Principal of Pakistan Islamia Higher Secondary School, Sharjah.

Arabic language is taught from class: I to Class: 12 to enable the students to learn the language of the Holy Quran and develop an understanding of the language and culture of the U.A.E. Islamiat is a compulsory subject at all levels to keep our students constantly aware of their religious obligations. The religious studies are essential for students at all levels as it is an important facet of life. It is vital that it is undertaken at the right age so that the value of religious beliefs, customs and traditions are imbibed early and are consequently followed in later years. Qirat competitions and Quran Khuwani are important features of our co-curricular activities.
